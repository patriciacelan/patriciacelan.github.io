The Lives of Pageant Queens

Prickly Thorns, Satin Dress
Teaser title only, real title to be unveiled at launch.

Although writing initially completed in 2020, this novel will be released as a prequel after Clinically Brunette. It shares the true story of overcoming a traumatic childhood to become a medical doctor.
"Vulnerability" in IJWPC
This essay on vulnerability in psychiatry won the 2024 McGill Programs in Whole Person Care Trainee Writing Award and was published under the journal citation:
Celan, P. (2024). Vulnerability in Psychiatry. The International Journal of Whole Person Care, 11(2), 25–29.
