Performing Arts

Patricia’s love for the performing arts began in childhood, though social anxiety held her back for years.
By gradually stepping outside her comfort zone through pageant talent competitions, medical school performance groups, and later online platforms, she built the confidence to share her art. Today, she has performed in numerous group productions and occasional solo acts, as a hobbyist singer, dancer, and acrobat.

Acrobatic Yoga
As a dedicated hobbyist, Patricia has taken her acro-yoga practice to 6 countries across 4 continents. Her current level is beginner-intermediate, so her performances are memorable more for their playful humour than technical perfection. Compilation videos are available on YouTube under Acroyoga Around the World. Her inspiring journey from an inability to do a headstand, all the way to international intermediate acro-yoga skills, has been documented on her Instagram account.
